In January 2017, I reached out to four people I admire about finding an excuse to do a project together. Dann Petty quickly replied, “Check your email.” Turned out he’d been designing a site for his upcoming documentary “Freelanced” and was ready to take on a partner who could turn his vision into a functioning website (he was producing, writing, shooting, editing...trying to do everything). Normally I’m the designer (unless I’m working w/ @lat who’s also a better designer than me) but I wasn’t going to pass up the opportunity to work w/ one of the best (designers/humans) I know. Dann’s making massive contributions to the design community and building really strong brands for himself in the process.
